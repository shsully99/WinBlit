What's your favourite children's poem?

The BBC are celebrating National Poetry Day by launching a poll to find the Nations' Favourite Children's Poem.


A special TV programme is going out on the eve of Poetry Day on BBC1 at 7.30pm on Wednesday October 3.

Presented by Zoe Ball it will include the results of a national poll vote to find the Nation's Favourite, as well as performances of some of the top ten poems from our poetry competition which has been judged by Spike Milligan. Zoe will be joined by Harry Enfield, Patsy Palmer and Steps.
