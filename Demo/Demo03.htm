From the WinBlit Window you will be able to read the text of the document using a speed reading technique known as Rapid Serial Visual Presentation (RSVP). The basic idea of RSVP is to bring the text to your eyes rather than in traditional reading where your eyes move to text. Some people like RSVP because it helps them to read more quickly and others because they find it a more comfortable way to read. We hope it serves as an assistant to those of you bogged down in your working life with information overload on your PC.

For an example of what RSVP appears like click the button with the   icon on the toolbar below.

Yes it is that simple. Text from whichever document are reading just gets flashed before your eyes. Though it may feel an unnatural way of reading at first with practise you may find that you can read more rapidly and that your comprehension will improve.
