Starting Speedreading

Either Click the   icon on the toolbar OR Select Start/Stop from the context menu  OR  press  the "S" shortcut key OR press the spacebar.

Pausing Speedreading

Either Click the  icon on the toolbar OR  Select  Pause from the context menu OR  press the "P" shortcut key OR press the control key and the spacebar simultaneously.

As on a media player Pause is effectively a toggle betweens playing and not playing (in our case reading and not reading).

Stopping  Speedreading

Either Click the  icon on the toolbar OR  Select  Start/Stop from the context menu OR  press the "S" shortcut key OR press the spacebar.

Changing Reading Speed

Either Click the  icon on the toolbar OR press <CTRL> and Up arrow to read faster OR <CTRL> and Down arrow to read slower.

There are many other functions and features you can learn by reading through the help file but you've just learnt the most important ones!
